Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

One 9-inch pie
30 Min - Prep time
10 Min - Cook time
40 Min - Total
8 Servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tbsp white sugar

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Make Lemon Filling: In a medium saucepan ...
    1. Whisk together 1 cup sugar, flour, cornstarch, and salt.
    2. Stir in water, lemon juice and lemon zest.
    3. over medium-high heat, stirring frequently, until mixture comes to a boil.
    4. in butter.
    5. Place egg yolks in a small bowl and gradually whisk in 1/2 cup of hot sugar mixture.
    6. Whisk egg yolk mixture back into remaining sugar mixture.
    7. Bring to a boil and continue to cook while stirring constantly until thick.
    8. Remove from heat.
    9. Pour filling into baked pastry shell.
  3. Bake: Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until meringue is golden brown.
